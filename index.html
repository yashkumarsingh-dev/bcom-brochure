About the Programme

B. Com (Honours / Honours with Research) Discipline Specific Electives in Financial Analytics, offered by Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Ranchi (Off Campus NOIDA) in collaboration with NSE Academy is a unique programme specifically designed as career-oriented programme which will enable students for suitable employment in Banking, Financial Institutions, FinTech institutions, NBFCs, Capital and Money Market firms and other organizations having substantial use of financial analytics in their decision making.

NSE Academy, a leading institution in financial education, brings unparalleled expertise and industry insights into the academic curriculum. This collaboration ensures that the programme is designed and delivered with input from top financial market practitioners, making the learning experience highly relevant and practical. Students will benefit from this collaborative programme and certification from NSE Academy Ltd. that are recognized across the financial industry.
